My Must Watch Anime According to Lukas

My Favourite Anime Tv Shows

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is animated beautifully and the jounery that tanjiro and nezuko
embark on so deep and meaningful, it never fails to have me on the edge of my seat.

Attack on titian

Great thrilling story that keeps blowing my mind with its plot twist.
Its crazy action scenes are some of the best animated scenes in anime in my opinion.

Cowboy bebop.

An amazing sci-fi/cyberpunk anime with alot of personality, it's funky characters
and amazing jazzy soundtrack, it always has me running back.
